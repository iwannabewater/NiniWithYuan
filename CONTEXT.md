# Nini & Yuan

`Nini & Yuan` is a Chinese-language fantasy adventure centered on two equal protagonists whose contrasting abilities and visual identities shape the same journey.

## Language

**Nini**:
The female protagonist, associated with starlight, gliding, precise movement, rose-toned visual motifs, and the Xuanji Star Dial. Her bearing is primarily graceful and airy, tempered by a smaller measure of youthful liveliness; her adult silhouette is light, flexible, and athletic at roughly 7.3 heads tall.
_Avoid_: Female Yuan, heroine duo

**Yuan**:
The male protagonist, associated with wind, dashing, direct movement, jade-cyan visual motifs, and the Jade Gui Sword. His bearing is primarily calm and protective, tempered by a smaller measure of youthful martial spirit; his adult silhouette is lean, stable, and moderately broad-shouldered at roughly 7.7 heads tall.
_Avoid_: Female Yuan, second heroine

**Protagonist pair**:
Nini and Yuan together as equal, entirely fictional young-adult leads, visually presented within the 20-to-24 age range, with a romantic bond expressed through the project's hidden personal layer. Their identities do not represent or preserve the likeness of real people.
_Avoid_: Single protagonist, heroine duo

**Xuanji Star Dial**:
Nini's signature celestial instrument, a carved astronomical artifact through which her starlight movement and magic are expressed.
_Avoid_: Magic wand, generic staff

**Jade Gui Sword**:
Yuan's signature artifact, a straight sword shaped by the proportions of a ceremonial jade gui and used to express his wind movement and force.
_Avoid_: Generic xianxia sword, folding fan

**Xuanji Union Seal**:
The product emblem formed by the circular Xuanji Star Dial, the vertical Jade Gui Sword, and rose and jade inlays representing the protagonist pair.
_Avoid_: Chibi couple icon, single-character app icon
