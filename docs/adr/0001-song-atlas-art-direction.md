# Adopt Song-atlas artifact fantasy as the visual direction

The product will replace its mixed Western night-atlas, magical-girl, action-RPG, and chibi treatments with one **Song-atlas artifact fantasy** direction across UI, characters, gameplay art, and application icons. Character clothing follows a seventy-percent Song-structure and thirty-percent celestial-fantasy balance: historically grounded garment construction carries the silhouette, while artifacts, carved ornament, gilt star markings, and lightweight protection carry the fantasy. High-detail character illustration uses Chinese gongbi-informed linework with semi-painterly rendering, adult facial structure, restrained eye proportions, and material-specific detail rather than glossy mobile-anime rendering or photographic realism. Adult appeal comes from proportion, tailoring, bearing, and material craft rather than designed exposure; the current idol-like bare midriff, short skirt, and ornamental excess are not retained. The interface uses a **Night Observatory** material base of black-indigo lacquer, deep indigo silk, moon-white text, aged gilt incision, carved jade, and restrained rose accents; depth comes from material changes, embossing, shallow relief, and wrapped gilt edges rather than generic gradients and glow. Ornament is concentrated rather than uniform: the primary frame, signature artifacts, brand emblem, and decisive states may be deeply worked, while ordinary controls remain quiet and reveal gilt or jade detail only through hierarchy and interaction. The signature interaction is **Gilt Tracing**: focus or confirmation briefly draws an aged-gold incision along the artifact edge, then settles a rose or jade inlay rather than illuminating the whole control. This direction preserves the existing celestial-map fiction while grounding its materials in Chinese astronomical charts, silk painting, lacquer, gilt linework, carved jade, and shallow relief; it was chosen over the higher ornament of Dunhuang-inspired imagery and the more generic austerity of xianxia ink painting because it best supports an elegant, crafted, readable interface on both web and Android.
